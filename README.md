# FocusRank – Stay Consistent & Stay Accountable

FocusRank is a **video-based study time tracker** that helps you log real study sessions, compete on leaderboards, and stay focused with built‑in Pomodoro & to‑do tools. No manual timers – we calculate your study duration directly from your uploads.

## ✨ Features

### 🎥 Video‑Based Study Tracking  
- Upload a raw study video **or** submit a YouTube link.  
- We automatically extract the video length → that becomes your study time.  
- No timers, no manual entries, no fake progress.

### 🏆 Healthy Competition  
- Daily, weekly & all‑time **leaderboards**.  
- Compare your performance with learners worldwide.  
- Category‑based rankings (subject, study style, etc.) – *your rank is your focus*.

### ⏱️ Lock Your Focus  
- **Pomodoro timer** for deep work sessions.  
- Integrated **to‑do list** to organise tasks.  
- Every focused minute earns ranking points – stay productive *and* climb the boards.

### 📊 Analytics & Insights  
- Daily and subject‑wise study statistics.  
- Personalized progress reports.  
- Streak tracking & reward system (coming soon).

## 🚀 Getting Started

1. **Sign up** – free account.  
2. **Upload a video** (raw file or YouTube link) after a study session.  
3. Watch your **time appear** on the dashboard.  
4. **Compete** on leaderboards, use the Pomodoro timer, and build your streak.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

**How does study tracking work?**  
We analyse the video length of your uploaded study session – that becomes your logged time. No manual start/stop.

**Do I need to record myself every time?**  
Yes, just record your actual study session (or a screen recording). The video proves the work.

**What kind of videos can I upload?**  
MP4, MOV, WebM (raw) or any public/unlisted YouTube URL.

**How do rewards and streaks work?**  
Consistent daily study earns streak bonuses; longer streaks unlock badges and featured spots on leaderboards.

**Is my data safe?**  
Yes – videos are stored encrypted, and we never share your personal information.

**Is the platform free to use?**  
Yes, the core features are free. Premium plans (advanced analytics, private leaderboards) may come later.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Frontend:** React + Tailwind CSS  
- **Backend:** Nodejs , Expressjs  
- **Database:** MongoDB
- **Video Processing:** FFmpeg / YouTube Data API  
- **Authentication:** JWT
